PLACES

Green Lakes State Park

Fayetteville, NY

Green Lakes State Park's outstanding features are its two glacial lakes surrounded by upland forest. Another special feature of the park is the 18-hole Green Lakes State Park Golf course.

Skaneateles

Skaneateles, NY

A clear blue lake alongside a vibrant downtown filled with unique shops and restaurants combine for a one-of-a-kind, relaxing experience.

Beak & Skiff Apple Farms Inc.

Lafayette, NY

Visit the #1 orchard in America. You can experience there many attractions including the Apple Barn, Apple Hill General Store, Beak & Skiff Bakery, and more.

Destiny USA

Syracuse, NY

Destiny USA is a six-story, automobile-oriented super-regional shopping and entertainment complex. It is the largest shopping mall in the State of New York.
